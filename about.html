About

I know that road.

My name is Gilbert Simba. Also called Morangi. I live and work in Kisii, Kenya. I take care of homes — landscaping, masonry, tiling, electrical, plumbing, painting. If it belongs to a building, I have probably fixed it.

You may call me a jack of all trades, but I consider myself the guy you reach to when trouble knocks. If something on your property needs attention, reach out.

But Wait a Minute!

That is not what this site is about. Here the workshop shifts. The arena is the human mind.

Like most people, I carried things quietly for a long time. Said fine and smiled when asked how I was doing. You know how that goes.

Then I noticed some people played the same game too. I felt something was off — or maybe life was not what I thought. So, I started to look inside. And the real journey began.

I built a few doors out of that journey. Each one named after something real.

I built them into QuietSpace because I did not want the next person to wait as long as I did. I am not a therapist. I am someone who was once where you might be now — and is still finding the way.

I built a door and left it open.

If something here moved you, go back and sit with it. The doors are always open. What you share inside stays between you and the space — I do not read your conversations and nothing is saved. If you want to reach me as a person, do not worry. I will know, and I will meet you half-way.

— Gilbert Simba, also known as Morangi
