About

I know that road.

My name is Gilbert Simba. Also called Morangi. I live and work in Kisii, Kenya. I take care of homes — landscaping, masonry, tiling, electrical, plumbing, painting. If it belongs to a building, I have probably fixed it.

You may call me a jack of all trades, but I consider myself the guy you reach to when trouble knocks. If something on your property needs attention, reach out.

But Wait a Minute!

That is not what this site is about. Here the workshop shifts. The arena is no more in the hands but in the human mind. Where ideas are hatched before the hands execute them.

Like most people, I carried things quietly for a long time. Said fine and smiled when asked how I was doing. You know how that goes.

Then I noticed some people played the same game too. I felt something was off — or maybe life was not what I thought. So, I started to look inside — the real journey began. The journey which led to something precious: QuietSpace.

QuietSpace is a website app that connects you towards your inner power — towards revealing your true self and healing as a consequence. So that the next person does not have to wait as long as I did. I am not a therapist. I am someone who was once where you might be now — and is still finding the way.

I built QuietSpace and left it open for someone like you and me.

If something is going to move you — and I know it will — sit with it. You will experience the purpose of this space. And if it feels right — share it with someone who needs it. QuietSpace is always open. What you share inside stays between you and the space — I do not read your conversations and nothing is saved. If you want to reach me as a person, do not worry. I will know, and I will meet you half-way.

— Gilbert Simba, also known as Morangi
